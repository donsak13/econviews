Macro commentary on Greek consumption usually starts with the national accounts and ends with retail trade indices. Both arrive with a lag of months. There is a faster signal, and it sits behind the counter of every licensed gaming agency in the country.

Why street-level GGR leads the official data

Discretionary spending on regulated gaming responds to household cash flow within days, not quarters. When disposable income tightens, ticket sizes shrink before supermarket baskets do.

A leading indicator is only useful if you can read it before everyone else. This one requires standing behind the counter.
