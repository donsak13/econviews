Analysis from inside the real economy.Ανάλυση μέσα από την πραγματική οικονομία.

I have spent 27 years operating inside Greece's regulated gaming sector — running a licensed agency in northern Athens through the boom years, the debt crisis, capital controls, and the digital transition of the market.

That vantage point shapes everything published here. Macro data describes the economy from above; a cash register describes it from below. The most useful analysis happens where the two views meet.

My current focus is the intersection of markets, regulation, and compliance: anti-money-laundering frameworks in the gaming sector, European capital markets structure, and the economics of Greek small business. I write in Greek and English, and I take on a limited number of advisory engagements.
